BROWN GIRL

Chapter 1

I was that girl 5’3" with big brown eyes,
long curly hair and luscious thighs.
had a smile that lit up my way,
but I was hurting at the end of the day.

Wearing lenghas, sarees, salwar kameez and anarkali’s,
my gold bangles shining a mile away.
My tikka shaping my face,
speaking Gujarati loud enough-
for the whole neighborhood to hear.
Embracing my Indian culture at last.

Why did I let my culture go?

Looking at myself in the mirror,
I thought, “who is this?”.
Where was the proud brown girl,
the same girl who once proudly embraced her culture?

I finally had the courage to put on a saree,
twirling around like a princess.
I felt everything was going to be okay again.
I will be able to get through this, whatever this was.

I walked out my room like a Bollywood princess.
My parents even complimented me,
and so did my siblings.
They saw a change in me,
and so did I

FIVE YEARS

Chapter 2

I miss you so much,
you have no idea.
I think about you,
even though I know you don’t think about me.
I wish nothing but the best for you.

I wish I didn’t lose you,
because you were my best friend.
You dealt with me and i was there for you,
but fate works in a weird way.

I deleted your number,
but you texted me three months later,
saying you were sorry.
I recognized your number right away.

You pushed me away multiple times,
but we always came back to one another.
This time it was different.
It felt different.
I felt different.

If you came back to my life again,
I would accept you with arms wide open.
Call me stupid,
but you were my best friend.
Even though you really broke my heart,
I’ll always forgive you.

I learned friends can break hearts too.

Everything between us is ruined,
but I forgive you.
I hold no grudge against you.
I wish for you to know that.

It’s time to put it all behind me,
all the laughing,
the crying,
late night talking,
stupid fights,
birthdays,
five years.
It's time to move forward,
to new beginnings

I knew I'd be okay,
with or without ya’.

One day out of the blue you text me,
saying you’re sorry.
I recognize your number right away-
and, as always I accept your apology.

I told my boyfriend about you and fought for your friendship,
only for you to turn your back on me again.
You’re back to your old ways again.

I’m tired of thinking that I did wrong,
when I haven’t done anything at all.
I’m tired of fighting for a friendship that’s one sided.

Finally I decided,
enough was enough.
It was my turn.
I blocked your number,
and haven’t looked back since.

TWELVE YEARS

Chapter 3

How can we be friends,
when I’m the only one that does all the talking?
My eyes light up when you barely say a couple of words.
I’m hoping you open up to me.

Everytime I confront you,
I feel like you lie to me,
or hide things from me,
to keep me on my toes.

You say you care about me,
you worry about me,
I mean something to you,
but i call bullshit.

I try and I try,
to show you I’m not like your ex,
or the last person you wanted to date,
but it’s like I fail every time,
and you don’t notice any of the things I do for you.

I saw you at your worst and-
still thought you were the best.

How can you say you appreciate me and-
the things I’ve done for you,
when you don’t show the appreciation?
When have you gone out of your way for me?

You just assume I’m fine,
and I’ll come talk to you.
You just walk away,
pretending that everything's okay.

In between the phone calls,
you coming by my desk,
visiting me,
calling me,
talking to me.
The late night calls,
sleeping over.
I started to like you,
and I knew that was a big mistake.

You told me you won’t put yourself out there for me,
but you’ve done it before with the wrong people.
It’s not like I treat you like garbage.

The biggest mistake I made,
was letting my pride down,
letting my ego down,
letting my cockiness down,
and telling you that I like you-
and I genuinely care about you.

Deep down inside,
I know that you know,
you hurt the one person God sent to heal you.

You asked for space because you had a lot of things going on.
You needed to figure things out.
Your behavior was different.

I would text you and ask you if you were okay.
your one word replies made me not want to text you anymore.

You would talk to everyone around me,
except for me.

By that point,
I tolerated enough.
I gave you all the space in the world.
I didn’t bother texting you,
or looking your way.

No more checking up on you.
No more buying you anything.
No more worrying about you.
No more going out of my way for you.
It’s time I pretend I don’t care about you,
It’s for the best.

We found our way back to each other,
as always,
but this time,
everything was different.

For some odd reason,
I couldn’t let you go.

ZAYN

Chapter 4

My sweet baby boy,
you are the greatest thing that’s happened to me,
truly a blessing.

Your round eyes remind me of me.
Your curly hair reminds me of your dad.
Those chubby cheeks, that I could kiss all day.

I’m trying so hard,
to be a better person for you,
learning how to take care of you,
and hold you.
I try to soothe you when you cry,
forgive me if I don’t do a good job,
or get frustrated.

There are times when I feel,
like I’m a horrible mother to you,
when I get frustrated with you.

It upsets me when I can’t calm you down,
and the tears are running down your cheeks.
I’m sorry for hurting you in anyway.

I hope when you grow up,
you will realize everything I’m doing for you,
all the long hours,
not being home,
barely spending any time with you,
just to make money for your future.

And yes,
it does kill me,
not to hold you in my arms.

When you grow up,
I hope you realize how loved you are,
even though my family isn’t around.

You’ll have so many questions,
and in time I’ll tell you all my truths.

I pray that you don’t blame yourself,
because it’s not your fault.

Everything I do,
is for you,
for your future,
your happiness.

I hope I make you proud one day,
and you’re able to call me a good mom,
because, sweetie-
I’m doing my best,
and I know I’m far from perfect.

But you, my sweet boy,
are perfect in my eyes.

No matter what happens,
you’ll always have me,
right by your side.

I love you so much,
my sweet little angel.
